Butterbean Stew

Chorizo Butterbean stew

Ingredients

  • 500ml Chicken stock
  • 450g Diced chicken thighs
  • 200g chorizo
  • 2 tbsp Smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp Cummin
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Handful chopped Parsley
  • 1 tsp Cayenne
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 5 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 Large diced onion
  • 1 large diced bell pepper

Steps

  1. Render oil from chorizo in pan, then move chorzio to a bowl, leaving the oil
  2. Brown chicken thighs in same pan then remove to same bowl as chorizo
  3. Fry onions until soft and translucent
  4. Add peppers,garlic and spices. Cook until fragrant
  5. Mix chicken and chorizo back into pan
  6. Pour in stock and add parsley
  7. Add butter beans to stew
  8. Simmer until slightly thickened
  9. Serve in your favourite bowl with crusty bread if thats your vibe
